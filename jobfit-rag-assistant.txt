# 1. What is our Goal?

Not just to finish Week 1 and Week 2 notebooks.

The goal is to build **one resume-worthy project** while learning.

### Project: JobFit RAG Assistant

A system that:

* Takes a Job Description (JD)
* Takes your Resume
* Finds matching skills/projects
* Calculates similarity
* Identifies missing skills
* Summarizes JDs
* Answers questions about JDs
* Recommends which of your projects to showcase

Later:

* Store Resume + JDs in ChromaDB
* Add RAG retrieval
* Add LLM prompting
* Add Function Calling
* Add Hallucination prevention
